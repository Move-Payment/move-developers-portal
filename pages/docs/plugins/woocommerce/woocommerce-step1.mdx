title: "Step 1: Access Plugin Settings in Woocommerce"
---

To configure the MovePay Payment Gateway plugin in WooCommerce, follow these steps:

1. Log in to your WordPress Admin Dashboard.
2. Navigate to **WooCommerce â†’ Settings**.
3. In the settings menu, go to the **Payments** tab.
4. Find **MovePay Payment Gateway** in the list of available payment methods.
5. Click on **Manage** next to the MovePay option to access the plugin's settings.

This will take you to the configuration page where you can input your MovePay credentials and customize the payment gateway settings.
