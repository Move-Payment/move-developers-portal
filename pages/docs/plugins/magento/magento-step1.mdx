title: "Step 1: Access Plugin Settings in Magento"
---

To configure the MovePay Payment Gateway plugin in Magento, follow these steps:

1. Log in to your Magento Admin Panel.
2. From the dashboard, navigate to **Stores** â†’ **Configuration**.
![Magento Configuration](/plugins/magento/configuration.png)
3. Under the **Sales** section, locate and select **Payment Methods**.
![Magento Payment Methods](/plugins/magento/payment-methods.png)
4. In the list of available payment methods, find **MovePay Payment Gateway**.
5. Click on **Configure** next to the MovePay option to access the plugin's settings.

This will take you to the configuration page where you can enter your MovePay credentials and customize the payment gateway settings.
